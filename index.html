Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Common Questions

1

What is a Fractional CFO?

A Fractional CFO is a senior financial expert who provides part-time or project-based strategic finance leadership — giving your business access to high-level financial guidance without the full-time cost.

2

How is Co-equiti different from an accounting firm or financial advisor?

Accounting firms focus on recording and compliance. Financial advisors often focus on investments.

Co-equiti, as a Fractional CFO, focuses on strategy — using your financial data to drive growth, improve profitability, and support long-term decision-making.

3

What is the difference between this and my accountant/bookkeeper?

The Accountant: "This is last month's profit and loss statement."

The Interim CFO: "Based on trends, here are the projections for the next quarter, and here's what we need to do differently to align with the strategy."

We complement your current accounting team, we don't replace it.

4

What systems/tools do we need?

We work with what you have. Accounting software: QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, Zoho Books, and others.

We provide our own software and analytical tools. No additional purchases required.

5

What happens if we need to pause the service?

We understand that the nature of business can be unpredictable. You can cancel your subscription with 30 days' notice. You can pause the service for up to 3 months (in exceptional circumstances) and resume at any time. No penalties or complications.

6

How do we measure success?

We will define success metrics together in advance:

  • Financial criteria: improved profitability, increased funding runway, successful fundraising
  • Strategic criteria: progress towards key objectives
  • Satisfaction: your confidence in financial management
